Your Student Robotics Blog feed If you enter the URL of your blog's RSS/Atom feed here, then other teams can read about your progress. You can enter a maximum of 1 blog feed but can change it using the box above. If you do not have a blog you can set one up for free at Blogger or Wordpress amongst many other sites. All feeds are verified by an SR mentor before appearing on the site, if your feed url has been verified, it will have a green background. If your feed has been checked and rejected it will have a red background.
